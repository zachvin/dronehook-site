How does giving help us?

Amount Product Description

$200 Pixhawk Pixhawks sit on the plane and handle all the connections between the drone's hardware components.
$300 Drone body We'll be testing our drone often, and the more drone frames we can buy in case we crash, the better.
$500 GoPro, resin 3D printer A GoPro allows us to record the flight from onboard the drone. A resin 3D printer gives us the opportunity to make stronger, higher quality, and more complex parts than a regular 3D printer.
